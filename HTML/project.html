Project

NRI Media, a Dubai-based company with experience of over 20 years in the media industry, is honouring distinguished Indian diaspora of the United Arab Emirates for contributing towards the growth of the nation we call our home today.


We are proud to announce the launch of our Collector's Edition of the Coffee Table Book titled - "UAE’s 100 Most Influential NRIs", to chronicle your success story. This limited edition Coffee Table Book will be unveiled shortly by an Indian government official under the patronage of the UAE Government.


Leading movers and shakers of society shall be honoured at the event in the presence of members of the UAW Minister, Indian Government officials and media personalities.


This limited edition Coffee Table Book will only be available at niche venues like consulates, associations, clubs, colleges, schools, airports, among others.
